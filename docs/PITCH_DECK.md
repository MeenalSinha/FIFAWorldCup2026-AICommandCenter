# Pitch Deck Content

## Slide 1 -- Title
FIFA World Cup 2026 AI Command Center
An AI Operating System for Smart Stadiums

## Slide 2 -- Problem
Stadium operations today are reactive: staff learn about a congestion
hotspot, a stockout, or a blocked accessible route only after it has
already become a problem for fans.

## Slide 3 -- Solution
A single platform where eight specialized AI agents continuously reason
over live stadium data and predict problems 15-45 minutes before they
happen -- across crowd safety, accessibility, sustainability,
transportation, and volunteer operations.

## Slide 4 -- Why generative AI, not just dashboards
Every module turns raw numbers into a worded, prioritized recommendation
via Gemini -- SOPs, route explanations, executive summaries,
multilingual fan support -- so staff act on language, not spreadsheets.

## Slide 5 -- Architecture
(See `ARCHITECTURE.md` diagram.) Next.js + FastAPI on Cloud Run, Gemini
for reasoning, Maps Platform for routing, Firestore for live state,
BigQuery for historical KPIs, Pub/Sub for agent-to-agent coordination.
